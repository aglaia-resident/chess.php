# remove

remove piece from board, example: `$chess->remove('e4');`

example:

```php
<?php
use \Ryanhs\Chess\Chess;

$chess = new Chess();
$chess->remove('e2');
echo 'FEN => ' . $chess->generateFen() . PHP_EOL;
echo $chess;
```

will return:

```

   +------------------------+

 7 | p  p  p  p  p  p  p  p |

 3 | .  .  .  .  .  .  .  . |

 1 | R  N  B  Q  K  B  N  R |
   +------------------------+
     a  b  c  d  e  f  g  h
```
